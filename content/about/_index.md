## About Codeverra

Codeverra is a learning platform focused on teaching Python, Data Structures, Algorithms, and AI with clarity and depth.

The goal is simple:
- build strong fundamentals
- develop problem-solving skills
- avoid shallow, shortcut-driven learning

---

## Teaching philosophy

- Concepts before code
- Intuition before optimization
- Structured thinking over rote memorization

This platform is built for learners who want to truly understand what they are doing.
